about us

Unselfish love of one's fellow men

MAA SANSTHA is a Charitable Non-Profit, NonGovernment Organization in India. People are our most valuable asset, the core principles at Maa Sanstha includes shared vision, goals, collaboration, sharing, mutual respect and joint investment of efforts in the overall goal of uplifting and empowering the under privileged.

The organization is actively engaged in social-welfare activities like Free Service of Cremation/Burial Service, Health Checkup & Blood Donation Camps, Education for Needy & Poor Children, Women Empowerment, Helping for Old-Age People and Made Shelter for them,Animal Shelter & Daily Feed System Etc.
